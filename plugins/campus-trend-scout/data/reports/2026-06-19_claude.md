# Daily US & Canada Campus Trend Brief
**Date:** 2026-06-19 | **Region:** US & Canada

## Today's Overall Theme

Two tracks today. From the news/trend side (Part A), the biggest pressure point for students is *access*: visa rules are cutting off the international pipeline into the US, Canada's permit cap is doing the same thing from the other direction, and the job market is only opening up for students who already did an internship. From the rankings side (Part B), the theme is *prestige vs. reality*: US News crowns Berkeley #1, but Princeton Review's student-experience data names a completely different #1 school for happiness, and a whole layer of themed rankings (professors, career services, food) never shows up in a prestige list at all.

## Top Topics

### #1 — US visa crackdown is cutting international student enrollment (38/40 score band, total_score 85)
New international enrollment fell 17% in fall 2025 as visa screening tightened (including social media review) and CPT/OPT oversight increased; 85% of institutions cite visa policy as their top obstacle to recovery, and graduate international enrollment is down 10,000 students year over year.
Sources: https://www.csmonitor.com/USA/Education/2026/0519/trump-visas-foreign-students , https://www.insidehighered.com/news/global/international-students-us/2026/01/07/how-colleges-hope-approach-international-higher-ed , https://www.highereddive.com/news/international-enrollment-under-pressure-what-colleges-can-do/812258/

### #2 — Entry-level hiring is loosening, but mostly for students who did internships (total_score 81)
NACE projects 1.6% YoY hiring growth for the Class of 2026; GPA screening has fallen from 73% to 42% of employers; CS grads with internships had pre-graduation offers at more than double the rate of those without; most growth is concentrated at small/mid-size employers.
Sources: https://www.naceweb.org/research/reports/job-outlook/2026/ , https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/new-grads-are-finding-jobs-faster-despite-a-competitive-job-market-says-report.html , https://www.collegerecruiter.com/blog/2026/05/14/10-signs-the-early-career-job-market-is-heating-up-in-may-2026

### #3 — Most college students now use AI for schoolwork, and a meaningful share cross into cheating (total_score 80)
A large Berkeley-led study finds 56% of undergrads use AI for assignments/exams, with at least 9% admitting to cheating and cheating rates varying by discipline; a separate survey puts AI tool usage among Gen Z students at 97%.
Sources: https://news.berkeley.edu/2026/05/21/the-largest-study-of-ai-use-by-undergrads-is-in-revealing-disparities-in-access-and-in-cheating/ , https://www.yahoo.com/news/startling-97-gen-z-students-162551464.html

### #4 — The pay gap between college majors keeps widening, and many grads regret their choice (total_score 76)
Research.com finds a $3.4M+ lifetime earnings gap between the highest- and lowest-paying majors; a national survey finds 39% of degree holders regret their major (44% in social/behavioral sciences).
Sources: https://research.com/degrees/highest-paying-college-majors , https://www.foxnews.com/us/major-regret-see-americans-feel-college-degrees-graduating.amp

### #5 — Each new graduating class is taking on more student debt than the last (total_score 72)
Average federal borrower debt is $39,547; Class of 2026 is projected to graduate with $43,500 average debt vs. $29,560 for the Class of 2024; the top 7% of borrowers hold 38% of all outstanding debt.
Sources: https://educationdata.org/student-loan-debt-statistics , https://getoutofdebt.org/243897/student-loan-debt-statistics-2026

### #6 — Roommate conflict is a near-universal but rarely-discussed part of the dorm experience (total_score 67)
Two student newspapers independently describe roommate conflict clustering early in the school year before boundaries are set, amplified by a recent Netflix film about roommates.
Sources: https://www.ubspectrum.com/article/2026/05/living-together-falling-apart-the-reality-of-roommate-conflict , https://www.idsnews.com/article/2026/04/netflixs-roommates-exacerbates-experience-of-roommate-collision

### #7 — Instagram still leads social use among college-age students, but TikTok dominates daily attention (total_score 65, needs_verification: true)
Pew Research finds 80% of 18-29 year-olds use Instagram and that 16-24 year-olds are the most digitally active age group, while TikTok far outpaces Instagram in daily usage frequency. Single-source for now; flagged for additional corroboration before full production.
Source: https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2025/11/20/americans-social-media-use-2025/

### #8 — US News #1 is Berkeley, but the #1 happiest students are at TCU (total_score 74) — Part B
US News 2026 ranks UC Berkeley #1 among public National Universities. Princeton Review's 2026 survey of 170,000+ students separately ranks TCU #1 for Happiest Students — and TCU isn't even a top-10 US News National University. Same ranking season, two different #1 schools, because the two rankings measure different things.
Sources: https://www.usnews.com/education/blogs/college-rankings-blog/articles/2025-09-23/college-rankings-2026-stability-and-strategic-updates , https://www.tcu.edu/news/2025/smile-the-princeton-review-ranks-tcu-at-the-top-for-happiest-students.php , https://www.fastweb.com/college-search/articles/top-10-happiest-colleges-in-the-country

### #9 — Princeton Review's themed rankings grade colleges on things US News never measures (total_score 67) — Part B
Princeton Review's 2026 themed categories name different winners for non-academic strengths: Sarah Lawrence College #1 for Professors, Bentley University #1 for Best Career Services, University of Richmond #1 for Student Support (also #2 Most Beautiful Campus, #3 Best-Run and Best Food) — none of which are top US News National Universities.
Sources: https://poetsandquantsforundergrads.com/news/princeton-reviews-2026-best-colleges-top-schools-for-professors-career-services-and-even-hard-liquor/ , https://urnow.richmond.edu/features/article/-/24906/princeton-review-names-ur-as-no.-1-for-student-support-no.-2-most-beautiful-campus-and-no.-3-for-best-run-and-best-food.html , https://www.fastweb.com/college-search/articles/top-10-happiest-colleges-in-the-country

### #10 — US News 2026 rankings actually moved this year, not just stayed stable (total_score 60) — Part B
US News changed its 2026 methodology (credit-hour-based expenditure calculation, SAT/ACT removed from some categories), producing real rank shifts: UCLA fell to #2 among public National Universities behind Berkeley, and in Regional Colleges Bentley rose to #1 as The College of New Jersey dropped from #1 to #4.
Sources: https://www.usnews.com/education/blogs/college-rankings-blog/articles/2025-09-23/college-rankings-2026-stability-and-strategic-updates , https://www.yahoo.com/news/articles/us-news-best-colleges-2026-215349352.html

### #11 — Canada's 2026 international student study permit cap is squeezing university admissions and budgets (total_score 74, added for AI-topic diversity)
Canada will cap 2026 study permits at up to 408,000 (7% below 2025, 16% below 2024); new undergrad international admissions are expected to fall nearly 50%; BC and Saskatchewan universities are already cutting programs in response.
Sources: https://www.insidehighered.com/news/global/2026/04/10/canada-stick-flawed-international-student-caps , https://themanitoban.com/2026/01/canadas-2026-international-student-cap/51250/ , https://globalnews.ca/news/10966102/international-student-cap-university-impact
